Custom Minigames

Experience exclusive minigames created by our development team including Melon Battle Royale, Parkour Paradise, and MelonWars. Compete with friends in rotating weekly challenges for special rewards.

Economy System

Our advanced economy features player-owned shops, dynamic market prices, and special limited edition items. Trade with other players using MelonCoins or collect rare artifacts to increase your server wealth.

Custom World

Explore our meticulously crafted world featuring custom biomes like Crystal Caverns and Melon Mountains. Discover 20+ hidden structures, legendary dungeons, and secret locations with exclusive treasures.

Events & Rewards

Join our weekly server events including Build Competitions, Boss Raids and Treasure Hunts. Win exclusive titles, cosmetics, and special permission sets that aren't available anywhere else on the server.

Custom Items

Wield powerful custom weapons like the Melon Slasher and Watermelon Shield. Discover over 50 unique items with special abilities that enhance your gameplay including enchantments exclusive to our server.

PvP Arenas

Battle in our custom arenas with unique mechanics like Gravity Wells, Lava Rises, and Power-up Stations. Participate in monthly tournaments with real prizes and earn a spot on our Champion's Wall of Fame.

Land Claims

Secure your builds with our enhanced protection system featuring visual claim borders, trusted friend lists, and customizable permissions. Expand your territory as you rank up with larger claim limits for premium members.

Custom Plugins

Enjoy custom-coded plugins like MelonPets, AutoFarm Systems, and Enhanced Crafting. Our development team regularly releases new features voted on by our community to ensure the best possible gameplay experience.

The ultimate Minecraft server experience with premium gameplay, unique features, and an amazing community.
