Server Rules

Follow these guidelines to ensure a fun and fair experience for everyone

Chat Rules

  • No Harassment

    Respect all players. Harassment, discrimination, or bullying based on race, gender, sexual orientation, religion, or any other factor will not be tolerated.

  • No Spamming

    Avoid excessive messaging, caps, symbols, or repetitive text that clutters the chat.

  • No Advertising

    Do not advertise other servers, websites, or services. Discord links are only permitted if directly related to your MelonMC gameplay.

  • Appropriate Language

    Keep conversations family-friendly. Excessive swearing, inappropriate topics, or adult content is prohibited.

Gameplay Rules

  • No Cheating

    Using hacked clients, mods that give unfair advantages, exploits, or any other form of cheating is strictly prohibited and will result in a ban.

  • No Griefing

    Intentionally destroying or altering other players' builds without permission is forbidden. Respect other players' creations and property.

  • No Lag Machines

    Intentionally creating devices or structures designed to cause server lag is prohibited and may result in a permanent ban.

  • Appropriate Builds

    All structures must be appropriate. Offensive or inappropriate builds will be removed, and the builder may face consequences.

Account Rules

  • One Account Per Player

    Alt accounts used to bypass bans or gain unfair advantages are not allowed.

  • Appropriate Usernames

    Usernames must be appropriate and not offensive. Staff may ask you to change your name if deemed inappropriate.

  • Account Security

    You are responsible for securing your account. Actions taken by others using your account are your responsibility.

Trading & Economy

  • No Scamming

    Dishonest trading, scamming other players, or failing to fulfill agreed-upon exchanges is prohibited.

  • No Real Money Trading

    Trading in-game items, currency, or services for real money is strictly forbidden.

  • Fair Trading

    Price gouging or market manipulation tactics that harm the server economy may be addressed by staff.

Important Notes

  • Staff has final say in all rule interpretations and enforcement.
  • Rules may be updated at any time. Check back regularly for changes.
  • Ignorance of rules is not an excuse for violations.
  • Report rule violations to staff through Discord or in-game using /report.
  • Penalties range from warnings to temporary or permanent bans depending on the severity and frequency of violations.

Appeals Process

Believe a moderation action was incorrect? Here's what to do

1

Wait 24 Hours

Before appealing, take 24 hours to reflect on the situation and understand the rule that was violated.

2

Join Discord

Join our Discord server if you haven't already.

3

Submit Appeal

Use the #appeals channel and follow the format provided in the pinned message.

4

Be Patient

Appeals are reviewed by staff members and may take up to 72 hours to process.
